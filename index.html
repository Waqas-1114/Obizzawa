Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services and process.

How long does a website project typically take?

Most standard website projects take 3-4 weeks from start to launch. This includes discovery, design, development, and revisions. Larger e-commerce sites or projects with complex functionality may take 6-8 weeks. I'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation based on your project scope.

What's your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing tailored to your needs. For website projects, we typically provide project-based pricing that reflects the scope, features, and level of customization required. For ongoing support or digital marketing services, we offer monthly retainer options designed to provide consistent, long-term value. Every project is unique, so we take the time to understand your goals and requirements before providing a detailed, transparent quote. This ensures you get a solution that fits both your business objectives and your budget—without any surprises.

Can you help with e-commerce websites?

Absolutely! I create custom e-commerce solutions. These include product setup, payment gateway integration, shipping configuration, and marketing strategies specific to online stores.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, I offer website maintenance packages to keep your site secure, updated, and performing optimally. These include regular updates, security monitoring, backup management, and technical support.

How do we get started working together?

The process begins with a consultation where we discuss your goals and requirements. After that, I'll provide a proposal outlining the scope, timeline, and cost. Once approved, we'll kick off with a detailed discovery session to gather all the information needed to start your project.
